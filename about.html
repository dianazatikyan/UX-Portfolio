About Me

Hello! I am a Los Angeles based designer with 5 years of experience and an extensive background in graphic design. My work aims to understand and empathize with the user, and my designs communicate solutions. I studied graphic design at USC and most recently received a certificate in UX/UI design from UC Berkeley.
