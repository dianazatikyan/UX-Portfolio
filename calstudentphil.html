Cal Student Philanthropy

Cal Student Philanthropy is a non-profit operating under UC Berkeley to annually raise money for senior gifts. Our team worked closely with Cal Student Philanthropy to redesign their current website in a manner which helped attract and retain users.

Starting Point

Current Site Analysis

Our redesign process commenced with a comprehensive analysis of the home page to identify areas of improvement. After careful consideration the following sections stood out:

1

Logo

The chosen typeface and colors do not visually connect the organization to UC Berkeley.

2

Main Navigation

The menu options lack intuitiveness, resulting in a disorganized navigation.

3

Donation Goal

The displayed donation goal appears to have been achieved, potentially decreasing the user's motivation to contribute.

4

Additional Logo

Text is small, vertical and pixilated, making it illegible to the user.

User Research

We interviewed 6 university alumni about their donations and alumni activities, and had them perform 3 tasks on the website.

Analysis

Following user interviews and testing, we utilized affinity diagrams, empathy maps, and a prioritization matrix to analyze our research findings.

Design

Our main focus was to visually align the website with UC Berkeley's brand aesthetic.

Testing

The prototypes underwent 9 rounds of testing, with interface adjustments made based on user feedback.

Empathy Phase

How does the user feel?

Users desired reassurance that the organization is a trustworthy and official partner of UC Berkeley.

Users expressed a desire to understand how their donation would make an impact and where it would go. Additionally, users encountered difficulty navigating the mission statement and found the organization's purpose unclear.

Getting Organized

Sitemapping

In an effort to enhance the navigation experience, we used card sorting to restructure the information architecture.

Finish Line

Final Prototype

The final prototype showcased the organization's affiliation with UC Berkeley by utilizing its colors and logo. The mission statement was relocated to the homepage to clarify Cal Student Phil's objectives.

A call-to-action (CTA) card on the homepage facilitated donations, and banners highlighted the organization's impact.
